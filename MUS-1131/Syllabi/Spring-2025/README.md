*Course Description:* Through guided listening in order to reach the orientation in musical, forms, historical development and aesthetics.

*Course Objective:* The course is designed to broaden the student’s appreciation for music by teaching deep-listening techniques. The class is focused on listening to enjoying and thinking about music. This class is a survey of western music history from 400 BC to the 20 CE and music nowadays in the local.

*Course Material:* *Text Book:* Ferris, Jean. Music, **the Art of Listening**. $7^{th}$ edition, McGraw-Hill – PDF.

*Class Schedule\**

Lesson 1 Introduction to Music Appreciation, and Timbre.  
Lesson 2 Timbre, Aesthetics, Reading.  
Lesson 3 Texture in music, and Rhythm  
Lesson 4 Timbre, Aesthetics, and Listening.  
Lesson 5 Review, Midterm Examination  
Lesson 6 Introduction to form, Part Forms  
Lesson 7 Integral Forms  
Lesson 8 Conflict in Music, Process  
Lesson 9 Pop music form, Ternary form.  
Lesson 10 Review, Final Examination
