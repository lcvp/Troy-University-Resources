### Guidelines for Group Reporting

1.  **Report Structure:** The report should be structured into four main sections:
    * **Introduction:** Overview of the topic and its significance.
    * **Theoretical Framework:** Relevant macroeconomic theories related to the topic.
    * **Empirical Analysis:** Analysis of real data and current trends.
    * **Conclusion & Recommendations:** Summary of key findings and policy suggestions.

2.  **Citations & References:** All theoretical concepts and data must be properly cited following the APA format.

3.  **Report Length & Formatting:**
    * The report should be between 15 to 25 A4 pages.
    * Use Times New Roman, size 12 for the main text.

4.  **Data Sources:** Recommended sources for data collection include:
    * General Statistics Office of Vietnam (https://www.gso.gov.vn/)
    * State Bank of Vietnam (https://www.sbv.gov.vn/)
    * Ministry of Finance and Investment (https://www.mof.gov.vn/)
    * United Nations Data (https://data.un.org/)
    * Trung tâm WTO và hội nhập (https://trungtamwto.vn/)
    * Other credible sources relevant to the topic.

5.  **Topic Selection:** Groups may narrow the assigned topic to a more specific focus. However, any modifications must be approved by the instructor to ensure alignment with the reporting requirements.
